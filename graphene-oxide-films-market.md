<p><p>Graphene oxide films are categorized into single-layer and double-layer films, each offering unique properties and applications. Single-layer films feature a single sheet of graphene oxide, providing high flexibility, electrical conductivity, and surface area, making them ideal for sensors and electronics. Double-layer films consist of two stacked sheets, enhancing mechanical strength and thermal performance, which is beneficial for energy storage and advanced coatings. Both types are pivotal in advancing nanotechnology and material science across various industries.</p></p>
