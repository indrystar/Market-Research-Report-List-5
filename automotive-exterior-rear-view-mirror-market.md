<p><p>The automotive exterior rear view mirror market is primarily divided into two types: electric adjustment and manual adjustment mirrors. Electric adjustment mirrors allow drivers to change the mirror angle using electronic controls, providing convenience and precision. These are increasingly popular in modern vehicles for their ease of use and integration with other electronic systems.
