<p><p>The Semiconductor and IC Packaging Materials Market encompasses various materials essential for protecting and connecting semiconductor devices. Leadframes serve as the structural foundation, supporting the chip and enabling connections. Ceramic packages offer high thermal and electrical performance for advanced applications. Solder balls facilitate connections between packages and PCBs. Other materials include adhesives, encapsulants, and die-attach materials, each playing a critical role in ensuring reliability and performance.</p></p>
