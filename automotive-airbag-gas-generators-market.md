<p><p>The automotive airbag gas generator market features various types, each utilizing different chemical compounds for deployment. Guanidine nitrate gas generators utilize guanidine nitrate, which can be combined with other compounds for efficient gas production. Additionally, "Others" encompass alternative materials and innovations aimed at enhancing airbag deployment effectiveness and environmental safety.</p></p>
