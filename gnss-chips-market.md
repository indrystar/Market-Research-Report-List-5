<p><ul><li>High Precision GNSS Chips</li><li>Standard Precision GNSS Chips</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GNSS chips market is divided into two main types: high-precision and standard precision GNSS chips. High-precision GNSS chips provide superior accuracy, often utilizing techniques like differential GNSS and Real-Time Kinematics, making them ideal for applications requiring pinpoint location, such as surveying and autonomous vehicles. Standard precision GNSS chips, on the other hand, deliver adequate accuracy for everyday applications like navigation and smartphones, catering to consumer needs without the complexity or cost associated with high-precision solutions.</p></p>
