<p><p>The heat exchanger tubing market consists primarily of two types: seamless tubes and welded tubes. Seamless tubes are produced from solid round billets and offer superior strength and pressure resistance, making them ideal for high-temperature applications. In contrast, welded tubes are manufactured by joining flat metal pieces together, allowing for more versatile designs and cost-effective production. Both types cater to various industries, including HVAC, power generation, and oil and gas, addressing specific thermal management needs and efficiency requirements.</p></p>
