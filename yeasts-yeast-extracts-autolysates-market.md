<p><p>Yeasts, yeast extracts, and autolysates are essential components in various industries, primarily in food, beverages, and pharmaceuticals. Autolysates are produced through the self-digestion of yeast cells, enriching them with proteins, vitamins, and amino acids.</p><p>The market for yeasts, yeast extracts, and autolysates is witnessing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for natural ingredients in the food and beverage sector, alongside rising consumer preferences for plant-based products and clean label solutions.
