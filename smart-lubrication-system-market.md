Single-line lubrication systems use a single line to distribute lubricant to multiple points, offering simplicity and efficiency. Dual-line systems, featuring two separate lines, allow for continuous lubrication even during maintenance. Multi-line lubrication systems provide targeted distribution to various lubrication points, enhancing precision.
