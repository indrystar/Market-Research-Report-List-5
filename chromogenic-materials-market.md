<p><p>The chromogenic materials market includes three primary types: photochromic, thermochromic, and electrochromic materials. Photochromic materials change color in response to light, often used in sunglasses and optics. Thermochromic materials shift color with temperature variations, commonly found in temperature-sensitive packaging and products. Electrochromic materials alter their hue when an electric charge is applied, utilized in smart windows and displays. Each type serves unique applications across industries, enhancing functionality and aesthetics in various products.</p></p>
