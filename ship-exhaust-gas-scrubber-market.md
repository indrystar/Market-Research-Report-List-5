<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Ship Exhaust Gas Scrubber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Loop Scrubbers</li><li>Closed Loop Scrubbers</li><li>Hybrid Scrubbers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ship exhaust gas scrubber market consists of three main types. Open loop scrubbers utilize seawater to remove sulfur oxides from exhaust gases, discharging the treated water back into the ocean. Closed loop scrubbers use freshwater mixed with a caustic solution, recycling the water without discharging pollutants, thus minimizing environmental impact. Hybrid scrubbers combine both systems, allowing ships to switch between open and closed loop operations, adapting to varying regulations and environmental conditions while ensuring compliance with emissions standards.</p></p>
