<p><p>The EPM (Ethylene-Propylene Monomer) rubber market encompasses various production methods, notably solution polymerization and suspension polymerization. Solution polymerization allows for better control over molecular weight and composition, resulting in high-quality rubber with desirable properties for automotive and industrial applications. In contrast, suspension polymerization offers a more straightforward process, producing rubber with larger particle sizes and good processability, making it suitable for products like tires and seals. Both methods cater to diverse performance requirements and market demands.</p></p>
