<p><ul><li>PIR (Passive Infrared) Sensors</li><li>Ultrasonic Sensors</li><li>Dual-technology Occupancy Sensors</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vacancy sensors market comprises various types, including PIR (Passive Infrared) sensors that detect motion through changes in infrared radiation emitted by occupants. Ultrasonic sensors use sound waves to detect movement, offering wider coverage. Dual-technology occupancy sensors combine both PIR and ultrasonic technologies for enhanced accuracy and reduced false triggers. Other types include microwave sensors that use electromagnetic waves and photoelectric sensors that detect light levels. Each type serves distinct applications in energy management and building automation.</p></p>
