<p><p>The oil and gas storage and transportation market encompasses various types of energy carriers. PNG (Piped Natural Gas) is delivered through pipelines, providing a continuous supply. LNG (Liquefied Natural Gas) is cooled to liquid form for shipping, optimizing transport over long distances. CNG (Compressed Natural Gas) is stored under high pressure for localized use. ANG (Adsorbed Natural Gas) utilizes adsorbent materials to store gas at lower pressure. NGH (Natural Gas Hydrates) involve gas trapped in ice-like structures, while others may include hybrid technologies and alternative fuels.</p></p>
