<p><strong>The Self-Healing Composites Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intrinsic Self-healing Material</li><li>Ambush Self-healing Material</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The self-healing composites market comprises two primary types: intrinsic self-healing materials and ambush self-healing materials. Intrinsic self-healing materials possess built-in mechanisms that automatically repair damage through chemical reactions or phase changes, ensuring durability and longevity. In contrast, ambush self-healing materials are designed to react to environmental triggers or damage, releasing healing agents when exposed to specific conditions. Both types aim to enhance the lifespan and performance of materials across various industries, from aerospace to construction, by reducing maintenance needs.</p></p>
