<p><p>Switching PTC thermistors are utilized in two primary market types: Self Heating Mode and Sensor Mode. In Self Heating Mode, these thermistors manage current flow to limit overheating, effectively serving as resettable fuses. In contrast, Sensor Mode employs the thermistors for precise temperature sensing and monitoring, capitalizing on their resistance changes with temperature variations. Both modes enhance safety and efficiency in various applications, including consumer electronics, appliances, and industrial equipment, ensuring optimal performance and protection against thermal hazards.</p></p>
