<p><p>The Medium Density Polyethylene (MDPE) resins market encompasses two primary production processes: the autoclave process and the tubular process. The autoclave process involves polymerization under high pressure within large reactors, resulting in resins with uniform molecular weight and excellent mechanical properties. In contrast, the tubular process utilizes continuous flow and involves polymerization in vertical reactors, offering enhanced control over the molecular structure and higher production efficiency. Both methods cater to diverse applications, including packaging, pipes, and construction materials.</p></p>
