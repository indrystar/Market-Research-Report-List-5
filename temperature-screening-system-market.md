<p><p>The Temperature Screening System market is divided into two main types: Mass Screening and Precise Screening. Mass Screening is designed for rapid assessment of large groups, making it ideal for public environments like airports or gatherings. It utilizes thermal imaging technology to identify individuals with elevated temperatures quickly. Conversely, Precise Screening focuses on accuracy for individual assessments, often employed in healthcare settings where detailed evaluation is necessary. This method typically involves handheld devices or specialized equipment for more reliable temperature readings.</p></p>
