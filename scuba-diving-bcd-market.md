<p><p>The scuba diving BCD market consists of three primary types: jacket type, back-inflate, and wing type. Jacket type BCDs offer balanced buoyancy and ease of use, wrapping around the diver for comfort and stability. Back-inflate BCDs position the air bladder on the back, allowing for streamlined swimming and minimal drag. Wing type BCDs feature a more simplified design, ideal for technical divers, providing excellent lift and maneuverability, particularly when paired with a harness and backplate system.</p></p>
