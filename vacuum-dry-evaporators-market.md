<p><ul><li>Rotary Evaporator</li><li>Heated Evaporator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vacuum dry evaporators market comprises two primary types: rotary evaporators and heated evaporators. Rotary evaporators utilize a rotating flask to create a thin film of liquid, enhancing evaporation efficiency under reduced pressure, making them ideal for sensitive compounds. Heated evaporators, on the other hand, employ heat to accelerate the evaporation process, effectively removing solvents from materials. Both technologies cater to various industries, including pharmaceuticals, food, and chemical manufacturing, focusing on efficient solvent recovery and concentration processes.</p></p>
