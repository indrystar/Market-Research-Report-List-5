<p><strong>The Magnetic Level Gauges Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Non Contact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Magnetic level gauges are essential for measuring liquid levels in various industries. The market is divided into two types: contact and non-contact gauges. Contact magnetic level gauges directly interact with the liquid, utilizing buoyancy principles to provide accurate readings. In contrast, non-contact magnetic level gauges use magnetic fields to detect liquid levels without direct contact, making them suitable for corrosive or hazardous materials. Both types offer reliability and precision, catering to diverse operational needs and enhancing safety in liquid measurement.</p></p>
