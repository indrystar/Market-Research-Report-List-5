<p><p>The Power Liftgate Motor market comprises two main types: DC Brushed Motors and DC Brushless Motors. DC Brushed Motors are traditional, using brushes for commutation, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness but with higher maintenance needs due to wear. In contrast, DC Brushless Motors rely on electronic commutation, providing greater efficiency, longer lifespan, and quieter operation, making them increasingly popular in modern applications. Both types are essential for automotive liftgate mechanisms, enhancing convenience and functionality in vehicle designs.</p></p>
