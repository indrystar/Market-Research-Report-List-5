<p><p>The two-wheeler brake caliper market includes two primary types: single-piston and multi-piston calipers. Single-piston calipers use one piston to apply braking force, offering simplicity, cost-effectiveness, and ease of maintenance, making them common in entry-level and commuter bikes. Multi-piston calipers feature multiple pistons for enhanced braking performance, providing better heat dissipation, consistent pressure distribution, and improved stopping power, ideal for high-performance and premium motorcycles. Both types cater to varying rider needs, balancing affordability and advanced braking efficiency in the two-wheeler market.</p></p>
