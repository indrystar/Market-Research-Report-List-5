<p><p>Reconfigurable Optical Add/Drop Multiplexers (ROADMs) enhance optical networks by enabling dynamic management of wavelengths. In this market, Wavelength Selective Switches (WSS) facilitate flexible routing of multiple wavelengths, allowing efficient bandwidth allocation. Optical Channel Monitoring (OCM) provides real-time monitoring of optical signals for performance validation and maintenance. Variable Optical Attenuators (VOAs) adjust signal power levels to optimize performance, ensuring signal integrity. Together, these components enable scalable and efficient network designs, crucial for modern telecommunications infrastructure.</p></p>
