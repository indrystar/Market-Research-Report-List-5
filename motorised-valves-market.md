<p><ul><li>Ball Valves</li><li>Butterfly Valve</li><li>Knife Gate Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The motorized valves market features three main types: ball valves, butterfly valves, and knife gate valves. Ball valves offer quick shut-off with minimal flow resistance, ideal for on/off control. Butterfly valves are lightweight and provide good flow regulation, commonly used in large piping systems. Knife gate valves are designed for shut-off applications in media with high solid content, featuring a sharp blade that slices through materials. Each type serves specific industrial applications, contributing to efficient fluid control and automation.</p></p>
