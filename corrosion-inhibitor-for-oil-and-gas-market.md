They are effective in acidic environments and widely used due to their versatility and biodegradability. The **Inorganic Type** includes compounds like chromates, silicates, and phosphates, which create passive layers or react with metal surfaces to inhibit corrosion. These are often used in high-temperature or high-pressure conditions but may have environmental concerns. Both types are essential for protecting infrastructure in oil and gas operations.</p></p>
