<p><p>The adhesives and sealants market comprises various types, including water-based, solvent-based, hot-melt, and others. Water-based adhesives are eco-friendly and versatile, suitable for porous substrates. Solvent-based adhesives offer strong bonding but may pose health risks due to volatile organic compounds. Hot-melt adhesives are thermoplastic polymers that provide quick adhesion, ideal for packaging and assembly. Other types include pressure-sensitive adhesives and reactive adhesives, catering to specific applications in construction, automotive, and consumer goods, each offering unique benefits based on project needs.</p></p>
