<p><p>Pressure mode ventilators are essential medical devices used in respiratory care, categorized into two main types: intensive care ventilators and portable/transportable ventilators. Intensive care ventilators are designed for use in hospitals, providing advanced support to critically ill patients with precise control over air pressure and volume. In contrast, portable/transportable ventilators are lightweight, versatile devices suited for emergency transport and home care, ensuring patient support outside traditional hospital settings. Both types play crucial roles in managing respiratory failure and enhancing patient outcomes.</p></p>
