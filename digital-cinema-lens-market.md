<p><ul><li>Prime Lens</li><li>Zoom Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital cinema lens market primarily consists of two types: prime lenses and zoom lenses. Prime lenses have a fixed focal length, offering superior optical quality, wider apertures, and lighter weight, making them ideal for low-light conditions and creative shots. In contrast, zoom lenses provide variable focal lengths, allowing for versatile framing without changing lenses. This flexibility is particularly beneficial in dynamic shooting environments. Both lens types cater to different filmmaking needs, influencing choices based on specific project requirements.</p></p>
