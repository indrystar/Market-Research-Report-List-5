<p><ul><li>Hot Rolled Deformed Bars.</li><li>Cold Rolled Steel Bars.</li><li>Mild Steel Bars</li><li>Twisted Steel Bars</li><li>Welded Steel Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The concrete reinforcing steel bars market comprises various types, each serving distinct purposes in construction. Hot rolled deformed bars feature surface deformations for better bonding with concrete. Cold rolled steel bars offer higher tensile strength and superior surface finish. Mild steel bars are known for their ductility and formability, suitable for low-stress applications. Twisted steel bars enhance mechanical interlocking with concrete, while welded steel wire provides flexibility and ease of installation in reinforced concrete structures, catering to diverse construction needs.</p></p>
