<p><p>Simplified Voyage Data Recorders (VDR) are essential devices used in maritime transport to enhance safety and accident investigation. The market for VDRs features two main types: Float Free Capsule and Fixed Capsule. Float Free Capsules are designed to detach and float away from the vessel during emergencies, ensuring data retrieval even if the ship sinks. Fixed Capsules, on the other hand, are securely mounted aboard the vessel and rely on the ship's structure for protection. Both types serve to safeguard critical voyage data.</p></p>
