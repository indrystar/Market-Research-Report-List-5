<p><p>The Ceramsite Sand market is categorized into three main types based on shape: gravel-shaped, spherical, and cylindrical. Gravel-shaped ceramsite features irregular, angular particles, providing excellent drainage and stability in construction applications. Spherical ceramsite offers enhanced flowability and packing density, making it ideal for lightweight aggregates in concrete. Cylindrical ceramsite boasts a robust structure, suitable for various industrial applications due to its uniform shape, which enhances performance in filtration and lightweight construction materials. Each type serves unique requirements across different industries.</p></p>
