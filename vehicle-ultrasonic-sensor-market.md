<p><p>A vehicle ultrasonic sensor is a device used in automobiles to detect obstacles and measure distances using ultrasonic waves. These sensors are commonly integrated into advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) for functions like parking assistance, blind-spot detection, and collision avoidance. They emit high-frequency sound waves that bounce off nearby objects, and the time taken for the echo to return is used to calculate distances, ensuring safer and more efficient driving.</p><p>The Vehicle Ultrasonic Sensor Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for safety features, the rise of autonomous vehicles, and stricter government regulations on vehicle safety.
