<p><p>Automotive wiper systems are essential for maintaining visibility during adverse weather conditions. There are two main types of wipers: bone wipers and boneless wipers. Bone wipers feature a metal framework that supports the wiper blade, providing sturdy performance and easy blade replacement. In contrast, boneless wipers utilize a sleek, streamlined design without a visible support structure, offering improved aerodynamics and aesthetic appeal. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, with bone wipers often favored for durability and boneless for modern styling.</p></p>
