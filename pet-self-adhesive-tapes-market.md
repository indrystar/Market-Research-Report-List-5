<p><p>The PET self-adhesive tape market encompasses various types, primarily categorized into water-based and oil-based adhesive tapes. Water-based adhesive tapes utilize a polymer emulsion system, offering good adhesion while being environmentally friendly and easy to clean. In contrast, oil-based adhesive tapes use solvents as binders, providing exceptional durability and resistance to high temperatures, making them suitable for demanding applications. Both types cater to diverse sectors, including packaging, electronics, and construction, addressing varying needs based on adhesion strength and environmental considerations.</p></p>
