<p><p>The compact benchtop centrifuge market is primarily categorized into ventilated and refrigerated centrifuges. Ventilated centrifuges utilize airflow to dissipate heat generated during operation, making them suitable for standard applications where temperature control is not critical. In contrast, refrigerated centrifuges maintain low temperatures, essential for sensitive samples, such as biological materials that require preservation from degradation. Both types cater to diverse laboratory settings, balancing efficiency and specific temperature requirements to enhance research and clinical outcomes.</p></p>
