<p><p>The construction laser market encompasses three main types: laser levels, laser scanners, and laser trackers. Laser levels are primarily used for measuring horizontal and vertical alignment during construction projects, ensuring precise leveling. Laser scanners create detailed 3D models of environments, aiding in design and planning by capturing accurate spatial data. Laser trackers offer high precision in measuring large structures, allowing real-time monitoring and adjustments, making them essential for quality control and assembly processes in complex construction tasks.</p></p>
