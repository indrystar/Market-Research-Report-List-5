<p><ul><li>Shielded</li><li>Unshielded</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multi-conductor cable market is primarily divided into shielded and unshielded types. Shielded cables are designed with protective coverings, providing immunity against electromagnetic interference (EMI), making them ideal for environments with high electronic noise. They ensure signal integrity and are commonly used in industries like telecommunications and industrial automation. In contrast, unshielded cables lack this protective layer, making them cost-effective and suitable for low-interference environments. They are often used in applications where shielding is unnecessary, like simple data transmission in controlled settings.</p></p>
