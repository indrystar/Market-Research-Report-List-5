<p><p>Column mechanical palletizers are categorized into low-level and high-level types based on their design and stacking capabilities. Low-level palletizers operate closer to the ground, allowing for easier loading and unloading of products without the need for extensive elevation adjustments. In contrast, high-level palletizers stack products at greater heights, optimizing warehouse space and automating the storage process. Both types enhance efficiency in material handling, catering to different operational needs in various industries, from food and beverage to pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
