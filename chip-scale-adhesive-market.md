<p><p>The Chip Scale Adhesive market includes three primary types: Insulation Type, Sintered Type, and Heat Curing Type. Insulation Type adhesives are designed to provide electrical isolation and thermal stability, making them suitable for sensitive electronic components. Sintered Type adhesives involve a process where materials are fused together under heat, enhancing adhesion and mechanical strength. Heat Curing Type adhesives require elevated temperatures to achieve optimal bonding performance, offering excellent durability for high-performance applications in electronics and semiconductor packaging.</p></p>
