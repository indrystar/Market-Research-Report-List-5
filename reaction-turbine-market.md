<p><ul><li>Axial Flow</li><li>Radial Flow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The reaction turbine market is primarily divided into axial flow and radial flow types, each defined by their flow direction. Axial flow turbines allow water to flow parallel to the turbine shaft, optimizing efficiency in low-head applications, and are commonly used in hydroelectric power generation. Conversely, radial flow turbines facilitate water flow perpendicular to the shaft, offering high efficiency in high-head situations. Both market segments cater to varying industry needs, influencing designs based on operational conditions and energy generation requirements.</p></p>
