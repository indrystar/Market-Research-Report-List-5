<p><p>The e-nose market comprises several types, including QMB/SAW, conducting polymers, and metal-oxide sensors. QMB (Quartz Microbalance) and SAW (Surface Acoustic Wave) sensors detect volatile compounds using frequency changes. Conducting polymer sensors rely on the conductivity change in response to target gases. Metal-oxide sensors utilize metal oxides to sense gas concentration through resistance variation. Other emerging technologies include electrochemical and acoustic sensors. Each type has unique applications in food quality, environmental monitoring, and healthcare diagnostics.</p></p>
