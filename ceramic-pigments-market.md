<p><ul><li>Simple Compound Type</li><li>Solid Solution-Oxide Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The **Ceramic Pigments Market** includes **Simple Compound Type** and **Solid Solution-Oxide Type** pigments. **Simple Compound Type** pigments are single-phase materials with uniform chemical compositions, offering consistent color properties and stability. They are cost-effective and widely used in traditional ceramic applications. **Solid Solution-Oxide Type** pigments are complex, multi-phase materials formed by blending metal oxides, providing enhanced color diversity, thermal stability, and durability. These are preferred for advanced ceramics, offering superior performance in high-temperature and demanding environments. Both types cater to diverse industrial and artistic ceramic needs.</p></p>
