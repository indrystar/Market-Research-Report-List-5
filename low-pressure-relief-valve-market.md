<p><p>The Low Pressure Relief Valve market is segmented into Type I and Type II. Type II valves, on the other hand, are engineered for more complex systems, accommodating higher flow rates and dynamic conditions, thus providing enhanced safety features and reliability. Both types serve crucial roles in various industries, ensuring equipment safety and optimal performance under low pressure scenarios.</p></p>
