<p><p>The body fat measurement market consists of various techniques, including Bioimpedance Analyzers (BIA), which assess body fat through electrical resistance; Skinfold Calipers, which measure skinfold thickness at specific body sites; Hydrostatic Weighing (HwD), a water displacement method; Air Displacement Plethysmography (ADP), which uses changes in air pressure to determine body volume; and Dual Emission X-ray Absorptiometry (DEXA), which provides detailed body composition analysis using low-level X-rays. Each method offers distinct advantages, targeting different consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
