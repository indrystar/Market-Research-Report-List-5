<p><ul><li>Single Beam Scanning Sonar System</li><li>Multi-beam Scanning Sonar System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The scanning sonar system market is primarily divided into two types: single-beam and multi-beam systems. Single-beam scanning sonar utilizes one focused beam to gather data over a narrow area, making it ideal for specific applications like depth measurement. In contrast, multi-beam scanning sonar employs multiple beams simultaneously, providing a broader and more detailed view of underwater environments. This allows for enhanced mapping and object detection, making multi-beam systems suitable for complex marine surveys and search operations.</p></p>
