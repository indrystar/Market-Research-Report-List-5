<p><strong>The Fly Ash Cement Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Class F Fly Ash</li><li>Class C Fly Ash</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fly ash cement market is primarily divided into two types: Class F and Class C fly ash. Class F fly ash, derived from burning anthracite or bituminous coal, has low calcium content and is primarily used in high-strength concrete applications and as a pozzolanic material to enhance durability. Class C fly ash, produced from lignite and sub-bituminous coal, contains higher calcium levels, enabling it to self-cement and support a broader range of construction applications, including structural fill and lightweight concrete.</p></p>
