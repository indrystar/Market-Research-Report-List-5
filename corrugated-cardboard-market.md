<p><p>Corrugated cardboard is a durable and lightweight material made from a fluted corrugated sheet sandwiched between two flat linerboards. It is widely used for packaging due to its strength, versatility, and eco-friendliness, as it is recyclable and biodegradable. The material is commonly used in industries such as e-commerce, food and beverage, electronics, and retail for shipping, storage, and product protection.</p><p>The Corrugated Cardboard Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the rising demand for sustainable packaging solutions and the rapid expansion of e-commerce. Additionally, the shift toward automation in packaging processes and the development of water-based adhesives and inks are shaping the industry. As sustainability becomes a priority, the demand for recyclable and biodegradable corrugated cardboard is expected to rise further, driving market expansion.</p></p>
