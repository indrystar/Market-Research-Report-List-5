<p><p>The 2D laser cutting machines market features two primary types: CO2 and fiber laser machines. CO2 2D laser machines use a gas mixture to generate a beam, ideal for cutting non-metal materials like wood, plastic, and textiles, offering versatility and precision. In contrast, fiber 2D laser machines utilize solid-state technology, producing a focused beam suitable for cutting metals such as steel and aluminum with high efficiency and speed. Each type serves specific applications, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
