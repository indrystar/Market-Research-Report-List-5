<p><ul><li>Storage</li><li>Non-storage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric water heaters market is broadly divided into two types: storage and non-storage systems. Storage water heaters, often referred to as tank heaters, maintain a reservoir of hot water for immediate use. These units are efficient for homes with high hot water demand. In contrast, non-storage water heaters, such as tankless or on-demand models, heat water directly as needed, eliminating the need for a storage tank. This offers energy savings and continuous hot water but may require larger initial investments.</p></p>
