<p><strong>The Salt Bath Furnaces Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrically Heated Salt-Bath Furnaces</li><li>Gas-Fired Salt-Bath Furnaces</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The salt bath furnaces market consists of two main types: electrically heated and gas-fired salt-bath furnaces. Electrically heated salt-bath furnaces utilize electrical resistance to heat the salt bath, providing precise temperature control and minimal emissions. In contrast, gas-fired salt-bath furnaces rely on combustion of gas to achieve high temperatures, offering quick heating times and cost efficiency. Both types are employed in various heat treatment applications, including hardening, tempering, and annealing, due to their effective thermal conductivity and uniform heating capabilities.</p></p>
