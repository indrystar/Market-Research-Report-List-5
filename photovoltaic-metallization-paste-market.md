<p><p>The photovoltaic metallization paste market primarily serves two types of solar cells: BSF (Back Surface Field) and PERC (Passivated Emitter Rear Cell). BSF solar cells enhance efficiency by reflecting unabsorbed light back into the solar cell, improving overall energy capture. PERC solar cells utilize an additional layer that reduces electron recombination at the rear, further boosting performance. Both technologies rely on high-quality metallization pastes to create effective electrical contacts, influencing the overall efficiency and production costs in the solar industry.</p></p>
