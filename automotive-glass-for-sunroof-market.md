Tempered glass is heat-treated for increased strength and safety, shattering into small pieces upon impact. Laminated glass, composed of layers bonded with a protective interlayer, offers enhanced security and noise reduction while preventing shattering. The "others" category includes specialized materials or technologies that may not fit standard classifications, such as panoramic sunroofs or advanced coatings for UV protection. Each type addresses specific consumer preferences and safety standards.</p></p>
