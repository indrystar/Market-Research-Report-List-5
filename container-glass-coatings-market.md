<p><p>The container glass coatings market is primarily divided into water-based and oil-based types. Water-based coatings utilize water as a solvent, offering lower environmental impact and easier cleanup, making them suitable for sustainable practices. They provide good adhesion and flexibility. On the other hand, oil-based coatings contain organic solvents, providing superior durability and resistance to chemicals and abrasion. They are often preferred for applications requiring robust protection and longevity, despite having environmental concerns. Each type serves distinct applications in packaging and consumer goods.</p></p>
