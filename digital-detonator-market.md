<p><ul><li>Lnstantaneous Electric Detonator</li><li>Delay Electric Detonator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital detonator market consists of two primary types: instantaneous electric detonators and delay electric detonators. Instantaneous electric detonators provide immediate ignition upon receiving a signal, enabling precise control in blasting operations. In contrast, delay electric detonators allow for timed detonation sequences, enhancing safety and effectiveness by creating controlled blast patterns. Both types leverage advanced digital technology to improve reliability and reduce misfires, making them essential in industries such as mining and construction for optimized blasting performance.</p></p>
