<p><ul><li>One-component</li><li>Two-component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silicone liquid rubber market is primarily categorized into one-component and two-component types. One-component silicone liquid rubbers are pre-mixed and can cure without requiring additional components, simplifying application and storage. In contrast, two-component silicone liquid rubbers consist of a base and a curing agent that must be mixed before use, allowing for customizable properties and increased versatility. Both types are widely used in various industries, including automotive, electronics, and healthcare, due to their excellent durability and thermal stability.</p></p>
