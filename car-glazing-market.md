<p><p>The car glazing market includes **Laminated Glass**, **Tempered Glass**, and **Polycarbonate** materials. Laminated glass consists of two glass layers bonded with a plastic interlayer, offering safety and noise reduction. Tempered glass is heat-treated for strength, shattering into small, blunt pieces upon impact, enhancing passenger safety. Polycarbonate is a lightweight, impact-resistant plastic alternative, ideal for weight reduction and design flexibility. Each type caters to specific automotive needs, balancing safety, durability, and performance in modern vehicle designs.</p></p>
