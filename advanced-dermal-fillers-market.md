<p><ul><li>Absorbable Fillers</li><li>Non-absorbable Fillers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The advanced dermal fillers market is categorized into absorbable and non-absorbable fillers. Absorbable fillers, primarily made of hyaluronic acid or collagen, are gradually broken down by the body, offering temporary results that typically last from several months to a couple of years. In contrast, non-absorbable fillers, often composed of materials like silicone or polymethylmethacrylate, provide long-lasting results but may pose greater risks of complications. Both types are used in aesthetic procedures to enhance facial volume, contour, and overall appearance.</p></p>
