<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Pressure Transmitters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absolute Pressure Transmitters</li><li>Differential Pressure Transmitters</li><li>Gauge Pressure Transmitters</li><li>Multivariable Transmitters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pressure transmitters market comprises various types catering to specific measurement needs. Absolute pressure transmitters measure pressure relative to a perfect vacuum, while differential pressure transmitters assess the difference between two pressure points, often used in flow measurements. Gauge pressure transmitters measure pressure relative to atmospheric pressure, commonly employed in industrial applications. Multivariable transmitters consolidate multiple measurements, such as pressure, flow, and temperature, into one device, offering enhanced data accuracy and efficiency for complex systems in various industries.</p></p>
