<p><p>The Sensor Signal Conditioner (SSC) ICs market is divided into resistive and capacitive types, catering to diverse applications. Resistive SSC ICs are utilized for sensors like potentiometers and thermistors, providing linear output by converting changes in resistance into voltage signals. Capacitive SSC ICs, on the other hand, are designed for sensors that measure changes in capacitance, such as pressure and proximity sensors. Both types enhance sensor accuracy, stability, and performance across industries including automotive, industrial automation, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
