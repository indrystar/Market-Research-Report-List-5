<p><strong>The Tailgate Liner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drop-in Bedliners</li><li>Spray-on Bedliners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tailgate liner market consists of two primary types: drop-in bedliners and spray-on bedliners. Drop-in bedliners are pre-formed plastic or rubber liners designed to be easily installed and removed, providing protection against scratches and impacts. In contrast, spray-on bedliners involve a customized application of a protective coating directly onto the truck bed, creating a seamless, durable layer that adheres to the surface. Each type offers distinct advantages in terms of installation, durability, and customization options for vehicle owners.</p></p>
