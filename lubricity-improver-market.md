<p><ul><li>Acidic Lubricity Improver</li><li>Non-Acidic Lubricity Improver</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lubricity improver market is primarily categorized into two types: acidic and non-acidic lubricity improvers. Acidic lubricity improvers enhance the frictional properties of fuels and lubricants by reducing wear and tear in metal surfaces through a reaction with metal oxides. In contrast, non-acidic lubricity improvers provide similar benefits without altering the pH, often offering greater stability in various operating conditions. Both types are critical in optimizing performance and extending the life of engines and machinery.</p></p>
