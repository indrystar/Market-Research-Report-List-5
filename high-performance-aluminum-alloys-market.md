<p><p>The high-performance aluminum alloys market is categorized into three main types: cast aluminum alloys, rolled aluminum alloys, and extruded aluminum alloys. Cast aluminum alloys are produced by pouring molten metal into molds, offering intricate designs and strength. Rolled aluminum alloys are obtained through processes that involve rolling sheets or plates, providing versatility for various applications. Extruded aluminum alloys are formed by forcing aluminum through a die, resulting in complex shapes ideal for structural components. Each type serves distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
