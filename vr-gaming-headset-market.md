<p><p>The VR gaming headset market is primarily divided into two types: tethered VR headsets and standalone VR headsets. Tethered VR headsets connect to powerful gaming PCs or consoles, utilizing their hardware for high-quality graphics and immersive experiences, making them ideal for serious gamers. In contrast, standalone VR headsets are self-contained devices with built-in processing capabilities, allowing for untethered movement and ease of use without external devices, appealing to casual gamers and those new to virtual reality.</p></p>
