<p><strong>The Silicone Molding Compounds Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Press Molding</li><li>Injection Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silicone molding compounds encompass various manufacturing techniques, primarily press molding and injection molding. Press molding involves placing silicone material in a heated mold and applying pressure, allowing the compound to form and cure simultaneously. This method is efficient for producing larger, flat parts. In contrast, injection molding involves injecting heated silicone into a mold cavity under high pressure, enabling intricate designs and high-volume production. Both methods cater to diverse applications in industries such as automotive, medical, and consumer goods.</p></p>
