<p><strong>The OPGW (Optical Ground Wire) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Central Tube Structure OPGW</li><li>Layer Stranding Structure OPGW</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The OPGW (Optical Ground Wire) market primarily includes two main types: Central Tube Structure OPGW and Layer Stranding Structure OPGW. Central Tube Structure OPGW features optical fibers housed within a central tube, offering high fiber-count capacity and robust protection, making it suitable for long-distance applications. In contrast, Layer Stranding Structure OPGW incorporates fibers arranged in layers around a metallic core, providing flexibility and ease of installation, ideal for various overhead power lines. Both types enhance telecommunications while ensuring electrical grounding.</p></p>
