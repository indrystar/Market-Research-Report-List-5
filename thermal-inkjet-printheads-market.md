<p><p>The thermal inkjet printheads market consists of two primary types: fixed printheads and disposable printheads. Fixed printheads are integrated into printers and are designed for long-term use, offering consistent performance and the ability to handle high-volume printing needs. Conversely, disposable printheads are designed for single-use applications, allowing users to easily replace them when they wear out or clog. This disposable nature can enhance print quality and reduce maintenance, catering to both consumer and industrial printing demands.</p></p>
