<p><p>The litmus paper market comprises two main types: red and blue litmus paper. Red litmus paper is used to test basic substances, turning blue when exposed to alkaline solutions. In contrast, blue litmus paper is utilized to identify acidic solutions, turning red in their presence. Both types are essential in laboratories, educational institutions, and various industries for quick pH testing. Their demand is driven by the growing need for convenient and effective pH indicators in scientific and industrial applications.</p></p>
