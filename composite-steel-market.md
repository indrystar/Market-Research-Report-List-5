<p><strong>The Composite Steel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Explosion Bonded</li><li>Roll Bonded</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The composite steel market features two primary types: explosion bonded and roll bonded steel. Explosion bonded steel is produced by using controlled explosive forces to bond dissimilar metal sheets, creating strong and durable materials suited for high-stress applications. In contrast, roll bonded steel involves pressing two metal sheets together under high temperature and pressure, resulting in a solid bond. Both methods are essential in industries like aerospace and construction, where lightweight, strong materials are crucial for performance and efficiency.</p></p>
