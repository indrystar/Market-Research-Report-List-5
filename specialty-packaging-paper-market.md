Wood pulp fiber materials are derived from virgin or recycled wood, offering excellent durability and printability, making them ideal for high-quality packaging applications. Kraft paper, on the other hand, is made from wood pulp through a chemical process, resulting in strong, tear-resistant sheets, commonly used for packaging goods such as bags, boxes, and envelopes due to its cost-effectiveness and sustainability.</p></p>
