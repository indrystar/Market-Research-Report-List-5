<p><p>The Halogen-free Flame Retardant Epoxy Resin market encompasses two primary types: reactive type and additive type. Reactive type epoxy resins integrate flame retardant components into the resin's chemical structure, providing inherent fire resistance and improving performance at elevated temperatures. In contrast, additive type epoxy resins incorporate fire retardant additives into the resin matrix, enhancing its flame resistance without altering the base resin's properties significantly. Both types cater to diverse applications in electronics, construction, and automotive industries, prioritizing safety and environmental compliance.</p></p>
