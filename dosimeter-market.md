<p><strong>The Dosimeter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TLD Dosimeters</li><li>OSL Dosimeters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dosimeter market primarily features two types: TLD (Thermoluminescent Dosimeters) and OSL (Optically Stimulated Luminescence) dosimeters. TLD dosimeters measure ionizing radiation exposure by trapping energy in crystals, which is released as light upon heating. OSL dosimeters, on the other hand, use laser stimulation to release trapped energy from luminescent materials, allowing for precise measurement. Both types are essential in fields such as occupational safety, medical applications, and environmental monitoring, offering reliable radiation dose assessments for various industries.</p></p>
