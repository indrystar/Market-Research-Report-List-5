<p><p>The right-angle gear reducers market includes three primary types: bevel gear reducers, worm gear reducers, and helical gear reducers. Bevel gear reducers utilize conical gears to transmit power at a right angle, offering high efficiency and compact designs. Worm gear reducers employ a screw mechanism for high torque and low-speed applications, ideal for space-constrained settings. Helical gear reducers feature angled teeth for smoother operation and reduced noise, making them suitable for a wide range of industrial applications.</p></p>
