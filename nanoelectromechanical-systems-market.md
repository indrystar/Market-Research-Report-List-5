<p><p>The Nanoelectromechanical Systems (NEMS) market comprises several specialized components, each serving unique functions. Nano-tweezers manipulate small particles at the nanoscale, crucial for biological applications. Nano-cantilevers detect changes in mass or force, utilized in sensing technologies. Nano-switches enable rapid signal processing and control in electronic devices. Nano-accelerometers measure acceleration with high sensitivity, essential for automotive and consumer electronics. Lastly, nano-fluidic modules facilitate the manipulation of fluids on a nanoscale, advancing applications in biomedical and chemical fields.</p></p>
