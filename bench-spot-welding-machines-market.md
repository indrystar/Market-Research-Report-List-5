<p><p>Bench spot welding machines come in three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and automatic. Manual welding machines require the operator to control the welding process, offering high flexibility for customized tasks. Semi-automatic machines streamline the process with some automated features, improving efficiency while still requiring operator input. Automatic welding machines provide full automation, allowing for high-volume production with minimal human intervention. Each type caters to different production needs, from small-scale operations to large manufacturing environments.</p></p>
