<p><ul><li>Synchronous</li><li>Induction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric AC motors market primarily features two types: synchronous and induction motors. Synchronous motors operate at a constant speed, synchronizing with the supply frequency, making them ideal for applications requiring precise speed control. Induction motors, on the other hand, are more widely used due to their simplicity and robustness; they operate using electromagnetic induction and generally run at a speed slightly less than the synchronous speed. Both types serve crucial roles in various industrial and commercial applications, contributing to energy efficiency and performance.</p></p>
