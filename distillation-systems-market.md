<p><strong>The Distillation Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>Distillation systems in the market can be categorized into several types based on their operational methods. Fractional distillation separates components based on boiling points using a column, ideal for high-purity products. Steam distillation utilizes steam to extract volatile compounds, commonly used in essential oil production. Vacuum distillation lowers boiling points to process heat-sensitive materials. Multiple-effect distillation (MED) enhances efficiency by using steam generated from previous effects. Other systems may include batch distillation and azeotropic distillation, catering to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
