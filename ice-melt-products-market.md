<p><p>The ice melt products market comprises two primary types: solid ice melters and liquid ice melters. Solid ice melters typically include granules or pellets made from materials like magnesium chloride, calcium chloride, or rock salt. They work by lowering the freezing point of water upon contact with ice. In contrast, liquid ice melters consist of solutions that can quickly penetrate and dissolve ice, offering faster action and reduced environmental impact. Both forms are essential for winter safety and convenience in varying applications.</p></p>
