<p><ul><li>Fixed Wing</li><li>Rotor Wing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inspection drones market is categorized into fixed-wing and rotor-wing types. Fixed-wing drones excel in covering large areas efficiently, making them ideal for extensive infrastructure inspections such as pipelines and power lines. They typically have longer flight times and greater range. Rotor-wing drones, or multirotors, are more maneuverable and suitable for detailed inspections in confined spaces, such as buildings and bridges, allowing for close-up imagery and real-time monitoring. Each type serves distinct inspection needs based on operational requirements.</p></p>
