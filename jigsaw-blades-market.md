<p><p>The Jigsaw Blades market is primarily divided into two types: T-Shank and U-Shank blades. T-Shank blades feature a tang that fits securely into the jigsaw, providing easier blade changes and better stability during operation, making them popular for professional use. U-Shank blades, with a U-shaped mounting, are typically found in older jigsaw models and offer less stability. T-Shank blades dominate the market due to their compatibility with modern tools, versatility, and superior performance in various materials.</p></p>
