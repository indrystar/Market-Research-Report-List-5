<p><p>The sandwich wrap packaging market includes various materials catering to different needs. **Plastic** wraps are cost-effective, durable, and moisture-resistant, ideal for short-term storage. **Kraft paper** is eco-friendly, biodegradable, and offers a natural look, often used for dry sandwiches. **Greaseproof paper** prevents oil seepage, maintaining freshness without sticking. **Wax paper** provides a moisture barrier, suitable for cold or dry items. **Foil paper** offers heat retention and insulation, perfect for warm sandwiches. **Aluminium foil** is versatile, providing excellent heat resistance and barrier properties for both hot and cold foods.</p></p>
