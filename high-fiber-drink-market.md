<p><p>The high fiber drink market comprises two main types: soluble fiber drinks and insoluble fiber drinks. Soluble fiber drinks contain fibers that dissolve in water, helping to regulate cholesterol levels and improve digestion. They often provide a smooth texture and sweet flavor, appealing to health-conscious consumers. In contrast, insoluble fiber drinks feature fibers that do not dissolve in water, promoting digestive health by adding bulk to stools and aiding regularity. Both types support overall gut health and wellness.</p></p>
