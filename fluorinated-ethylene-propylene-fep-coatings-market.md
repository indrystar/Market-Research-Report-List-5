<p><p>Fluorinated Ethylene Propylene (FEP) coatings are specialized materials known for their chemical resistance and non-stick properties. The market is divided into two main types: powder coating and liquid coating. Powder coating involves applying a dry powder that, when heated, melts and forms a solid, durable layer, ideal for industrial applications. Liquid coating, on the other hand, involves the application of a wet solution that dries to form a seamless, flexible film, suitable for more intricate surfaces and uniform coverage. Both types serve distinct needs across various industries.</p></p>
