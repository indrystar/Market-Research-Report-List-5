<p><ul><li>Regular Type</li><li>Instant Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The adult whole milk powder market is primarily categorized into two types: Regular and Instant. Regular whole milk powder is produced by evaporating milk and then spray-drying it, resulting in a product that requires reconstitution. It is commonly used in food production and can be used in cooking and baking. Instant whole milk powder, on the other hand, undergoes additional processing to enhance solubility, allowing it to dissolve quickly in water without clumping, making it convenient for direct consumption or use in beverages.</p></p>
