<p><strong>The Smart Card Integrated Circuit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Contactless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Card Integrated Circuit market is divided into two main types: contact and contactless. Contact smart cards require physical connection to a reader, typically utilizing gold-plated contacts for data transfer, ideal for secure authentication tasks such as banking and access control. Conversely, contactless smart cards use radio frequency identification (RFID) technology, allowing data exchange without physical contact, enhancing convenience in applications like public transportation and mobile payments. Both types prioritize security and rapid transactions in their respective use cases.</p></p>
