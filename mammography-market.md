<p><p>The mammography market encompasses various types of imaging technologies. Analog mammography uses film to capture images, providing a traditional approach. Digital mammography captures images electronically, allowing for enhanced clarity and easier storage. Computed radiography utilizes a photostimulable plate to convert x-rays into digital images. Digital radiography offers real-time imaging with immediate results, improving workflow. Breast tomosynthesis provides 3D imaging, enhancing detection rates by allowing radiologists to examine layers of breast tissue, reducing overlap and improving diagnostic accuracy.</p></p>
