<p><p>Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene (ABS) is produced using various methodologies, primarily classified into three market types: Emulsion Graft Copolymerization, Bulk Copolymerization, and Others. </p><p>Emulsion Graft Copolymerization involves synthesizing ABS in an aqueous medium, promoting improved impact resistance and providing a finer particle size. </p><p>Bulk Copolymerization, on the other hand, occurs in a molten state, enabling greater control over molecular weight and structure, resulting in enhanced mechanical properties. </p><p>"Others" encompasses alternative methods such as suspension polymerization and solvent-based techniques that cater to specific applications or requirements in the market.</p></p>
