<p><p>Vacuum Jacketed Pipe (VJP) is an insulation technology used to transport cryogenic fluids, such as liquefied natural gas (LNG) and liquid nitrogen. The design consists of an inner pipe surrounded by an outer pipe, with a vacuum created between them to minimize heat transfer and prevent boil-off of the cryogenic fluids. This technology is critical in applications requiring efficient and safe transportation of cryogens, particularly in industrial, medical, and research sectors.</p><p>The Vacuum Jacketed Pipe Market is poised for significant growth, driven by increasing demand in industries such as energy, aerospace, and healthcare.
