<p><p>The boat covers market consists of two primary types: woven and non-woven materials. Woven boat covers are made from interlaced fibers, offering durability, strength, and breathability, making them suitable for various weather conditions. In contrast, non-woven covers are composed of bonded fibers, providing water resistance and lightweight protection. While woven covers often provide better UV resistance and longevity, non-woven options are typically more cost-effective and easier to handle, catering to different customer needs in boat protection solutions.</p></p>
