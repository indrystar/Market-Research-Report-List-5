<p><p>The poultry disease diagnostic kit market is categorized into several types: Antigen-antibody tests, PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction), ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay), and others. Antigen-antibody tests detect specific proteins from pathogens, while PCR amplifies DNA to identify genetic material of diseases. ELISA measures antibody responses or antigens in samples, providing qualitative or quantitative results. Other diagnostic methods may include immunofluorescence and microarrays. These techniques are crucial for early disease detection, improving poultry health management and minimizing economic losses in the industry.</p></p>
