<p><p>The analytical laboratory instrument market comprises three primary types: Element Analysis, Separation Analysis, and Molecular Analysis. Element Analysis involves identifying and quantifying chemical elements in samples, often using techniques like spectroscopy. Separation Analysis focuses on isolating specific components from mixtures through methods such as chromatography. Molecular Analysis examines the structure and composition of compounds at the molecular level, utilizing techniques like mass spectrometry. Together, these categories enable comprehensive chemical characterization across various industries, including pharmaceuticals, environmental testing, and food safety.</p></p>
