<p><p>The extruded aluminum heat sink market comprises two main types: active and passive heat sinks. Active extruded aluminum heat sinks incorporate fans or other cooling mechanisms to enhance heat dissipation, making them suitable for high-performance applications where heat must be managed efficiently. In contrast, passive extruded aluminum heat sinks rely solely on natural convection to dissipate heat, making them ideal for low-power or quieter applications where minimal noise and lower energy consumption are prioritized.</p></p>
